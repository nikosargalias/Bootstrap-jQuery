Out response to COVID-19 ↡

What COVID-19 means for you and us

We're sure you've been following the development of the COVID-19 virus, and we want to let you know what our response is.

If you're studying with us: don't worry. We're doing everything we can to make sure your learning experiences with us won't be disrupted. Because most of our courses are offered flexibly online, you can continue studying as normal, wherever you're working from.

If you're an apprentice, any classroom-based learning has been moved to a blended model, which includes live sessions via Adobe Connect. Additionally, any meetings with learning and development coaches (LDCs) will now happen virtually rather than in person. Your LDC will be in touch to book this.

We are continuing to enrol new students and recruit great candidates for apprenticeships! We also want you to know that we have some free learning available to help you brush up and sharpen your skills. We’re confident we can find the right opportunity for you.

Find out more information on free learning, furloughed apprentices and breaks in learning here.
